The Bell Tower provided the perfect setting for Kristen and Guido’s romantic, flower-filled wedding reception. Decorated with delicate gold details, lush floral arrangements in hues of white and pink with verdant greenery, the soft glow of candles, and white draping from the beams, their celebration exuded elegance.

But before we dive into the splendor of their reception, let’s rewind to where it all began – the love story of Kristen and Guido. Childhood sweethearts, their bond stood the test of time and distance, only to be rekindled later in life.

Guido whisked Kristen away to his native country, Italy, where amid the breathtaking beauty of Lake Como, he asked her to be his forever with the help of his parents.

Fast forward to their wedding day, where the Bell Tower served as a symbol of their enduring love and the beginning of their new chapter together.

As guests entered the main space, they were greeted by cascading white drapery with a grand chandelier at the center, and rose-filled floral arrangements with the delicate scent of pink and white blooms mingling with the warm glow of candles.

During cocktail hour, guests sipped on signature cocktails— an Oaxaca Old Fashioned and Raspberry Moscow Mules —while eating an array of hors d’oeuvres made by the in-house culinary team. Selections included candied bacon, Key West crab cakes, and classic tomato bruschetta.

They then found their seats at tables set with elegant gold accents and a mix of smaller floral arrangements in bud vases, with textured pink napkins and custom menu cards at each place setting. They dined on a balsamic caprese salad and their choice of filet mignon, rigatoni primavera, or pan seared dayboat fish.

Amid laughter and heartfelt toasts, Kristen and Guido danced the night away, their love shining brighter than ever. Surrounded by family and friends, they celebrated not only their union but also the journey that brought them to this moment of joy and happiness.

As the evening drew to a close, the newlyweds left the Bell Tower hand in hand, ready to embark on the adventure of marriage together.

May Kristen and Guido’s love continue to bloom and flourish and their journey together be filled with countless moments of joy, laughter, and unwavering love!
