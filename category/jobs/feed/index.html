Infinity Hospitality Group is seeking reliable and dedicated Event Staff who have a real passion for providing next-level service at our venues,Â The Bell Tower and The Bridge Building.

Event staff positions are all-encompassing roles, and every team member will be cross trained to fill whichever positions are needed for each event, including greeting, bartending, serving, busing, ushering, and more.

Shifts are staffed on a first come first serve basis. This gives you the ability to make your own schedule. It is a perfect second job for those who need flexibility.

EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Part-time, Full-time
COMPENSATION: $17 per hour

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Greeting or ushering, buffet service, table service, passing appetizers, bartending, busing or clearing, general cleaning, and setup or tear down of events
  • Preparing banquet room, tables, and buffet as stated in banquet order or supervisor's instructions
  • Serving food and drinks per banquet orders
  • Making sure glasses are filled and clearing plates as needed
  • Communicating special meal requirements to the kitchen
  • Greeting, acknowledging, and thanking guests

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Prior experience as a banquet server (preferred)
  • Able to meet uniform requirements
  • Strong communication skills
  • Friendly attitude
  • Courteous manners

If this sounds like the type of work you are looking for, we are doing open interviews Wednesday to Friday from 10 a.m. - 4 p.m. at our office located at 345 Hill Ave. Nashville, TN 37210.

You can also apply by clicking the button below to email us at HR@infinityhospitality.net. Please attach your resume and include which position you're applying for. Let us know if you have any further questions or and would like to set up an interview.
