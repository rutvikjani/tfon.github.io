The Estate at Cherokee Dock was the perfect spot for a beautiful union of love and cultural fusion for Megan and Jorge last September for their modern Mexican wedding.

Megan and Jorge went to the same high school together but didn’t know each other until a few mutual friends introduced them years later. Little did they know that six years and one romantic lodge proposal later, they would walk this aisle together as husband and wife! 

They exchanged vows in front of the Estate with the serene lakeside views in the background and the willow oak canopy to their side. Wood crossback chairs sat in the lawn alongside the main drive making the perfect runway to the altar for Megan, escorted by her mom. 

Following the ceremony, Jorge’s cultural heritage was intricately woven in with mariachi melodies leading guests to a cocktail hour on the Lakeview Event Center patio, which was dressed with fairy lights. Here, modern flair met traditional Mexican charm as fun velvet lounge seating arrangements invited guests to relax and mingle with a glass of their signature passion fruit margarita. Vibrant colors of pink, orange, and more were incorporated into the decor, adding pops of color against the elegant backdrop of the estate. 

Megan and Jorge also set up an area for photos with a pink velvet couch underneath a wooden arch adorned with vibrant floral arrangements by LMA Designs. It was finished with a chili pepper garland and custom neon sign that said, “Te amo.”  

As the sun began to set, guests transitioned into the event center for the reception. The ambiance was one of refined elegance. Champagne-colored satin linens adorned the tables, creating a luxurious setting, and white floral centerpieces exuded sophistication while paying homage to Megan’s classic taste. Each table was set with a papel picado-inspired table number and custom white maracas for every guest.

During the reception, guests savored a plated dinner experience that combined gourmet delights of smoked brisket with grilled corn and a black eye pea salad, maple mustard glazed chicken with brown sugar carrots and roasted garlic mashed potatoes, and a rigatoni primavera. 

As the night came to a close, Megan and Jorge danced to “Ride With Me,” by Cody Johnson under the shimmering installation of disco balls and chandeliers for the first time as newlyweds.  

Their wedding at Cherokee Dock was not only a testament to their love but also a beautiful blend of cultures, creating memories to cherish for a lifetime.

Deysi and Michael celebrated their love with an enchanting garden wedding at the Estate at Cherokee Dock. With its picturesque landscapes, grand mansion, and lush gardens, the Estate provided the perfect backdrop for their magical day.

Deysi, originally from Brazil, and Michael, from New Jersey, first met at work when Deysi moved to New Jersey. Starting off as just friends, Michael showed Deysi the ropes when she made the big move. Their love grew slowly but surely, and after another move to Nashville, these two were ready to tie the knot.

With most of their guests coming from out of town, and many from Brazil, they wanted to make sure it was a smooth and enjoyable experience for all. It was important that the food, drinks, and atmosphere were top tier— and with us, that’s a promise! The Estate was the perfect backdrop for their guests to celebrate with its breathtaking views of Old Hickory Lake and sprawling lawns. A truly memorable and magical experience.

Deysi and Michael got ready with their own wedding parties in the mansion’s luxury bridal suite and nearby Stable Loft  before walking the aisle to say “I do” on the Lakeside Lawn. The lakeside ceremony was set with arrangements of vibrant blooms and lush greenery at the altar with a flower petal aisle.

Afterwards, guests sipped on glasses of frozé with a selection of passed apps during the outdoor cocktail hour in the Serenity Gardens, including Bloody Mary oysters, hot chicken biscuits, ahi tuna in a crispy wonton, arancini, polenta cakes with goat cheese, and Key West crab cakes. Lounge areas were also set to create a comfortable environment for guests to mingle with string lights for a fairytale ambiance. 

For their reception inside the Lakeview Event Center, Deysi and Michael blew their guests away with the most stunning and enchanting indoor garden design. The room was draped in forest green velvet with strings of fairy lights across the whole ceiling, while tables were set with soft white linens and acrylic ghost chairs. Centerpieces included antique candelabras wrapped in greenery and full-size faux trees with floating glass bulbs, individually hung white flowers, tea lights, and touches of moss. It was like a fairy tale brought to life!

For dinner, we helped Deysi and Michael curate a culinary experience that embraced Nashville’s renowned food scene. The menu featured a fusion of Southern flavors and personal favorites with a selection of four entrees—lemon almondine trout, grilled lamb chop with a tomato demi-glace, chicken cordon bleu, and rigatoni primavera.

During the reception, live musicians played celebratory tunes to kick off the dance floor  and encourage loved ones to join the couple in the fun. And after a late night dessert display of French toast bread pudding, mini cheesecake bites, cake cutting, and more, Deysi and Michael ended their night with a jump in the pool!

Tala and Vagif said their “I dos” at the Estate at Cherokee Dock with a stunning outdoor ceremony in the Serenity Gardens followed by a beautiful seated dinner in the Lakeview Event Center. 

These two first met during their college years and eventually fell in love. Vagif proposed at Nashville’s Love Circle, a romantic spot that overlooks the entire city. After surprising Tala with a sweet picnic, she said yes!

When it came to planning their wedding, it was important for the couple that their day flowed flawlessly so their only focus could be on sitting back, relaxing, and taking it all in. Accomplishment achieved, if we say so ourselves! Their day went off without a hitch.

Tala stunned in a sweetheart mermaid gown with draped off-the-shoulder sleeves and embroidered lace details. She accessorized with a jeweled hair piece, dainty tennis necklace, and a waltz length veil. Her bridesmaids donned champagne floor-length gowns in different styles, and all the ladies carried matching long-stem bouquets of classic white florals and greenery. Vagif and his groomsmen looked dapper in classic black tuxedos with coordinating white floral boutonnieres.

Following intimate vows in the Serenity Gardens, guests gathered on the patio of the Lakeview Event Center for a cocktail hour featuring an array of hors d’oeuvres prepared by the in-house chefs. Dishes included  ahi tuna in wonton baskets, avocado toast, butternut goat cheese stuffed mushrooms, classic tomato bruschetta, mini beef wellington, and braised short ribs. 

When the reception began, Tala and Vagif were introduced with a special cultural performance by the Al Fursan Dabka and Zaffah Group as guests prepared for a plated dinner service of chicken piccata with grilled asparagus and parmesan risotto and herb salmon with broccolini. 

The space was set with the most beautiful classic design featuring a mix of neutral white and champagne-colored linens, round and rectangle tables, and light wood crossback chairs. The tables were decorated with beautiful floral arrangements full of white blooms and greenery, white taper candles in gold candlesticks, and some elevated flowers to create dimension across the room. String lights were also hung above, cascading to the center of the room to create a warm glow. 

After a sweet first dance to Elvis Presley’s “I Can’t Stop Falling In Love,” Tala and Vagif cut their classic white three-tier cake and opened up the dance floor!

Abigail and Jacob said their “I dos” with a stunning outdoor ceremony on the Lakeside Lawn at the Estate at Cherokee Dock, followed by a beautiful seated dinner in the Lakeview Event Center.

The couple first met through a mutual friend in 2020. On their first date, they slow-danced in an empty parking lot in the rain. Talk about romance! Abigail and Jacob both knew within a week they had something special and were engaged the following year.

Abigail stunned in a sweetheart mermaid gown with a large, timeless bow on the back. The couple exchanged their vows on the beautiful Lakeside Lawn, standing in front of a ceremony arch created with white florals and greenery, which served as a stunning backdrop with sweeping views of the lake behind them.

Following the ceremony, guests gathered in the Lakeview Event Center, which was decorated in all whites for Abigail and Jacob’s classic reception. The tables were set with wooden chairs, crisp white linens, stunning elevated white flower arrangements of roses and greenery, with white drapery cascading across the ceiling. The large covered patio was set with a round white bar and mixed seating, round white and cocktail tables also decorated in white, providing guests with the perfect setting to enjoy the evening.

After dinner, everyone enjoyed a night of dancing and entertainment alongside the beautiful lakeside views!

As their dreamy wedding day came to an end, Abigail and Jacob shared a memorable celebratory jump into the pool with their wedding party. They then drove away in a luxurious Bentley as fireworks went off down the scenic drive. A perfect ending to a classic fairytale of an evening!
