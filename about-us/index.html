ABOUT

About TFON

Founded in 2015 in Ahmedabad, India, Tfon Events specializes in quality decoration services for theme weddings, receptions, sangeet sandhya, mehendi, birthdays, baby showers, home decor, and more.

We partner with various party plots, banquet halls, gardens, and private venues to offer comprehensive decoration services. Our goal is to make your occasion a grand success and a memorable one.

For corporate events, we provide exceptional services to help you crack the best deals, expand your network, and leave a lasting impression. With extensive experience in corporate events, business meets, and celebrations of all sizes, we ensure top-notch service for every occasion.

Customized Invitations

TFON helps you make customized invitations for every occasion, including bridal showers, baby showers, graduation parties, and more.

Photography & Videography

Our simple yet sophisticated service means we only commission experienced event photographers and videographers and use the most advanced cloud-based technology to make your photos and videos available quickly and efficiently. We also offer Photo Booth hire with an assistant on hand for the duration of the event.

Celebrity Management

Our celebrity management service involves 360-degree celebrity and brand fit services including endorsement management, portfolio management, strategic PR, digital presence management, grooming, etiquette, appearance, etc.

Founded in 2011, Infinity Hospitality was created out of a love for dynamic events and fine dining. Our goal is to provide unparalleled event services and outstanding customer service.

Why Infinity? Because with us, there are infinite possibilities. Big or small, we are committed to creating one-of-a-kind events that your guests will remember for years to come. Customization is guaranteed, from your floor plan to your bar menu and signature lounge furniture.

We work with the best vendors in the Nashville area to provide planning and design services at the venue of your dreams, and we also offer full services at The Bridge Building Event Spaces, The Bell Tower, and The Estate at Cherokee Dock through exclusive venue management and in-house catering.
